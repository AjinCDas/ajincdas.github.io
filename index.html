01 About me

I'm a full stack web developer with 10 years of experience spanning software development and infrastructure. My journey began with 3 years of .NET MVC and SQL Server, followed by 7 years of PHP engineering across Drupal, Laravel and CodeIgniter — with Drupal as my core specialty, from Drupal 8 through Drupal 11.

Day to day I design and build multisite Drupal platforms: custom themes using Single Directory Components (SDC), paragraph-driven page building for editors, custom modules and field formatters, Webform integrations, AJAX-powered listings, and performance-focused CSS/JS architecture.

For the last 4 years I've also owned the DevOps side — Linux, Apache and Nginx, Docker & DDEV local environments, and Azure DevOps CI/CD pipelines with Azure App Service, VMs, File Storage and managed MySQL. I enjoy combining both disciplines to deliver reliable, scalable web solutions end to end.
