I'm a Senior Full Stack Drupal Developer with 10 years of experience — 3 years on .NET MVC and SQL Server, followed by 7 years of PHP engineering across Drupal, Laravel and CodeIgniter. Today I build enterprise and government platforms used across Qatar, including Qatar Free Zones, Qatar University, Qatar Rail and several ministries.

My core work is Drupal 8–11 multisite development — component-based themes with SDC, paragraph-driven page building for editors, and headless architectures serving APIs to Angular and React front ends. I also deliver AI-first: design-to-code from Figma via MCP, Claude-assisted CMS development, and Jira-connected AI workflows that clear bug fixes within sprint deadlines.

On the platform side I own the stack end to end — Linux, Apache and Nginx, Docker environments, and Azure DevOps CI/CD with App Service, VMs and managed MySQL. I'm equally at home shipping features and running the infrastructure they ship on, and I'm open to Platform Engineer roles.
